IT / ITES Pune, Maharashtra

Structured Cabling for 8,000-Seat IT Campus, Hinjewadi

Challenge

A leading software firm needed complete passive network infrastructure for a new campus — four 15-storey towers, 8,000 workstations, server rooms on each floor, and a central data centre — all delivered within a 16-week construction window.

Our Approach

Deployed 800km of Cat6A cabling across 48 IDFs, with OM4 fibre backbone connecting each IDF to the main MDF. Used structured containment (cable trays, J-hooks) for clean routing. All outlets tested and certified with Fluke DSX-8000 to TIA-568-C.2 standard.

800km Cable installed
16wk Delivery
100% Pass rate
Cat6A OM4 Fibre Fluke Certified
Banking & Finance Pan-India (50 branches)

SD-WAN Rollout Across 50 Bank Branches — Replacing Legacy MPLS

Challenge

A private sector bank was spending ₹2.4 crore/year on MPLS for 50 branches. Core banking latency averaged 180ms during peak hours. The IT team had no real-time visibility into branch WAN performance and was reactive to outages.

Our Approach

Deployed Fortinet SD-WAN with dual ISP (broadband + 4G backup) per branch. Application-aware policies prioritised core banking traffic. Zero-touch provisioning enabled rapid deployment — 2 branches/week without field engineers. Centralised NOC for 24×7 monitoring.

45% WAN cost reduction
62ms Avg latency (was 180ms)
99.9% Uptime achieved
Fortinet SD-WAN ZTP NOC
Healthcare Mumbai, Maharashtra

800-Camera IP Surveillance for Tertiary Care Hospital, Mumbai

Challenge

A 600-bed super-speciality hospital needed comprehensive CCTV coverage across all clinical, administrative, and public areas — including high-security zones (pharmacy, OT, ICU) with access control integration. Previous analog system had blind spots and no analytics.

Our Approach

Deployed 800 Hikvision 4K IP cameras with AI video analytics (crowd detection, intrusion alerts, smoke detection). Biometric access control at 120 critical doors. Integrated with hospital HR system for automatic access provisioning. NVR storage with 60-day retention.

800 4K cameras
120 Access control doors
60d Video retention
Hikvision 4K AI Analytics Biometric
Education Delhi NCR

Wi-Fi 6 Campus Rollout — 25,000 Concurrent Users, Delhi NCR University

Challenge

A 300-acre university campus had a decade-old Wi-Fi infrastructure (802.11n) that couldn't support the density of student devices. Classrooms dropped connectivity during exams. Outdoor areas had no coverage. The administration wanted seamless roaming for 25,000 users.

Our Approach

Predictive Ekahau RF design for every building and outdoor area. Deployed 1,200 Aruba Wi-Fi 6 APs on new Cat6A infrastructure. Per-user bandwidth policies via ClearPass. Seamless roaming across the entire campus. Dedicated IoT VLAN for 5,000+ smart devices.

1,200 APs deployed
25,000 Concurrent users
100% Campus coverage
Aruba Wi-Fi 6 ClearPass Ekahau Design
IT / ITES Chennai, Tamil Nadu

Tier III Data Centre Build — 500 Racks, Financial Services, Chennai

Challenge

A financial services firm needed to exit three aging co-location facilities and consolidate into a single owned Tier III data centre. Requirements included 2N power redundancy, sub-20ms latency to AWS Mumbai, and the ability to scale from 200 to 500 racks within 2 years.

Our Approach

Full DC design and build — including hot/cold aisle containment, 2N UPS and generator infrastructure, in-row precision cooling, MPO fibre spine-leaf cabling, and complete DCIM (Schneider EcoStruxure). Phased migration from co-lo with zero business disruption.

500 Rack capacity
40% Cost vs. co-lo
1.4 PUE achieved
Tier III 2N Redundancy DCIM Spine-Leaf

ServiceNow ITSM + ITOM Implementation — 15,000-Seat Tech Firm, Bangalore

Challenge

A 15,000-seat IT services company was managing incidents via email and spreadsheets. MTTR averaged 6 hours. Their CMDB was nonexistent — no asset visibility, no service mapping. An upcoming client audit required ITSM maturity evidence within 90 days.

Our Approach

Implemented ServiceNow ITSM (Incident, Problem, Change, CMDB) + ITOM Discovery. Used MID Server for on-premise discovery. Populated full CMDB with 45,000 CIs in 8 weeks. Automated 60% of routine incidents via Flow Designer. Delivered user training to 500+ IT staff.

60% MTTR reduction
45,000 CIs in CMDB
8wk Go-live
ServiceNow ITSM ITOM CMDB Flow Designer
