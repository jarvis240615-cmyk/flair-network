Structured Cabling

A well-designed cabling infrastructure is the invisible backbone of every enterprise network. At Flair Network Systems, we design and deploy enterprise-grade structured cabling systems that exceed TIA-568, ISO/IEC 11801, and BICSI standards. From small office deployments to sprawling multi-building campuses, our cabling installations deliver consistent performance, simplified management, and the flexibility to support evolving technology requirements for decades.

We work with Cat6A, Cat7, and fibre optic solutions (single-mode and multi-mode) tailored to your bandwidth and distance requirements. Our team handles end-to-end delivery: site surveys, design, installation, testing, certification, and as-built documentation.

  • Cat6A & Cat7 copper cabling — voice, data, PoE
  • Single-mode & multi-mode fibre optic (LC/SC/MPO)
  • Structured horizontal and backbone cabling
  • Patch panel & cable management design
  • Fluke DSX-8000 certification testing
  • As-built drawings and full documentation
  • Data centre structured cabling (cable trays, containment)
  • Ongoing MAC (moves/adds/changes) support

SD-WAN Networks

Traditional MPLS networks are expensive, inflexible, and can't keep up with cloud-first enterprises. Our SD-WAN solutions give you application-aware routing, centralised management, and internet-circuit cost savings of 40–60% compared to legacy WAN architectures. We partner with Cisco Viptela, Fortinet SD-WAN, and VMware NSX to deliver the right fit for your network topology and security requirements.

Whether you're connecting 5 branches or 500, our SD-WAN deployments deliver consistent, predictable performance for SaaS applications, real-time voice and video, and private data centre traffic — all with zero-touch provisioning and single-pane-of-glass visibility.

  • Zero-touch provisioning for branch rollouts
  • Integrated next-gen firewall and IPS
