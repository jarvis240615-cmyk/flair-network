Each industry has unique infrastructure demands, compliance requirements, and operational constraints. Here's how we address them.

01

IT & ITES

Software, GCC, and BPO environments demand ultra-high-density networks with 100% uptime for business continuity. We've built infrastructure for India's largest IT parks.

Key Challenges
  • High-density seating (500–10,000+ workstations per floor)
  • 24×7 operations with zero planned downtime tolerance
  • Rapid capacity expansion as headcount grows
  • Complex multi-vendor tool landscape (ITSM, ITOM)
  • Data centre efficiency and PUE targets

02

Banking & Finance

Banks and NBFCs operate under strict RBI regulations with zero tolerance for downtime. Our branch networking and DC solutions are built for the financial sector's unique compliance demands.

Key Challenges
  • Multi-site branch connectivity (50–500+ locations)
  • RBI cybersecurity and infrastructure compliance
  • Core banking system latency requirements
  • Physical security at cash-handling areas
  • Legacy MPLS replacement with cost reduction
Our Solutions
SD-WAN Security & CCTV Structured Cabling IT Audits ServiceNow
03

Healthcare

Hospitals and diagnostic centres have unique cabling requirements — plenum-rated materials, infection control considerations, and integration with clinical systems like PACS, EMR, and nurse call.

Key Challenges
  • High-bandwidth medical imaging (PACS, CT, MRI)
  • Patient data privacy (HIPAA-equivalent) compliance
  • Fire-rated plenum cabling requirements
  • Critical area surveillance (pharmacy, ICU, OT)
  • Roaming Wi-Fi for clinical staff on mobile devices
Our Solutions
Structured Cabling Wi-Fi Solutions Security & CCTV Access Control AV Systems
04

Education

Universities and schools increasingly depend on technology for delivery — from Wi-Fi for thousands of simultaneous students to smart classrooms and distance learning infrastructure.

Key Challenges
  • Thousands of concurrent Wi-Fi users (students + IoT)
  • Wide campus coverage including outdoor areas
  • Smart classrooms and lecture capture systems
  • Campus-wide security and access management
  • Budget constraints with long-term infrastructure needs

Wi-Fi 6 Structured Cabling AV Systems Security & CCTV SD-WAN
05

Manufacturing

Factory floors require industrial-grade networking that withstands harsh environments — heat, vibration, dust, and EMI interference — while supporting Industry 4.0 IoT and automation systems.

Key Challenges
  • OT/IT network segmentation (SCADA isolation)
  • Industrial-grade Wi-Fi for AGVs and handheld scanners
  • Harsh environment cabling (dust, moisture, vibration)
  • Multi-plant WAN connectivity for ERP
  • Perimeter security for large industrial sites

Industrial Wi-Fi SD-WAN Structured Cabling Edge DC Security

Retail & Hospitality

Retail chains and hotel groups need consistent, high-quality connectivity across dozens or hundreds of locations — from POS systems and inventory management to guest Wi-Fi and digital signage.

Key Challenges
  • Consistent network across 50–500 distributed sites
  • Guest Wi-Fi with bandwidth management and branding
  • POS network reliability and PCI-DSS compliance
  • Digital signage and in-store AV systems
  • Real-time inventory and analytics visibility
