The Vision Behind
Every Network

Satish Kondalkar founded Flair Network Systems in 2004 with a conviction that Indian enterprises deserved IT infrastructure partners who truly understood their business — not just vendors who sold boxes and walked away.

His approach has always been to start with business objectives, design backwards to the infrastructure, and deliver with obsessive attention to quality and documentation. This philosophy has created an organisation where engineers think like business partners and project managers think like engineers.

"Technology is the foundation of every modern business, and its reliability plays a critical role in performance, security, and growth. We work closely with our clients as a trusted technology partner, delivering secure and scalable solutions. Every solution is designed with a clear focus on stability, efficiency, and long-term business value."

Under Satish's leadership, the company has grown from a two-person cabling shop to an 80+ engineer organisation serving Fortune 500 companies, leading PSUs, and high-growth startups — all while maintaining the responsiveness and personal attention of a boutique firm.
