Our Story

About Flair Network Systems

Two decades of building the IT infrastructure that powers India's most critical enterprises — from banks to hospitals, campuses to data centres.

Built on Trust. Delivered with Precision.

Flair Network Systems was established in 2004 with a singular vision: to provide enterprise organisations across India with world-class IT infrastructure that they could depend on. What began as a structured cabling specialist has grown into a comprehensive IT infrastructure company serving clients in banking, healthcare, government, manufacturing, and beyond.

Over the past two decades, we have deployed over 200,000 connections, supported 70+ long-term enterprise clients, and built a reputation for technical excellence, reliable delivery, and unmatched post-implementation support.

"Technology is the foundation of every modern business, and its reliability plays a critical role in performance, security, and growth. We don't just build networks — we build the backbone of businesses." — Satish Kondalkar, Founder & CEO, Flair Network Systems

Today, Flair Network Systems is ISO 9001:2015 certified and holds partnerships with industry leaders including Cisco, Aruba, Panduit, and Fortinet. We continue to expand our service portfolio — from SD-WAN and Wi-Fi 6 deployments to ServiceNow consultancy and data centre managed services.
